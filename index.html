Lakshya Goyal

I am Lakshya Goyal, a tech enthusiast currently pursuing my Master's in Machine Learning and Data Science at UC San Diego. I am currently exploring the rapid rise of large language models and translating that progress into dependable, real-world systems. My work includes contributing to the San Diego Supercomputer Center's Wildfire Smoke Detection initiative, where I build and evaluate open-source LLM and computer-vision pipelines with a focus on data engineering, inference efficiency, and observability. I also conduct quantitative neuroimaging research in UC San Diego's Hnasko Lab, developing segmentation and spatial mapping of Substantia Nigra neurons and deriving cell-to-boundary metrics. Previously, I served as a Software Engineer at Flipkart, where I delivered production features at scale and improved system performance and reliability.
